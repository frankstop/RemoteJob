# RemoteJob Product Context

register: product

## Product Purpose

RemoteJob is a public, static remote-work tracker. It helps job seekers scan fresh remote listings, filter quickly, track actions in local browser storage, and export their state without creating an account.

## Users

- General remote job seekers across role families.
- Applicants doing daily or batch applications.
- People who care more about tracking seen, saved, skipped, and applied jobs than another generic job-board feed.

## Principles

- Public pipeline, private user state.
- Source transparency before breadth.
- Dense enough for repeat daily use.
- Standard controls over novelty.
- Explainable fit scoring.

## Anti-References

- Marketing landing pages before usable app UI.
- Fake account features.
- Unclear scraping.
- Opaque AI scoring.
- Narrow role-family assumptions.
